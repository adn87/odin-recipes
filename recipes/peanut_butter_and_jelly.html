Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

Easy breakfast recipe for making a Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwich for 1. Takes up to 5 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon butter, softened
  • 2 slices bread

Steps

  1. Stir the peanut butter, honey, and butter together in a bowl until smooth; spread on 1 of the bread slices and sandwich with the other slice.
  2. Enjoy!!! :)
