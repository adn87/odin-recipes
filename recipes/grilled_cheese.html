Grilled Cheese

Description

Waking up feeling like having a grilled chees sandwich? Here's a grilled cheese recipe that can be made in 20 mins tops and has 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup figs
  • 1 cup shittake mushrooms
  • 4 slices Diets & Watson Havarti
  • 4 slices Dietz & Watson Yellow Cheddar
  • 4 slices Dietz & Watson Colby Jack
  • 4 slices hearth bread
